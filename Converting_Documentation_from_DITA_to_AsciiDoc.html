Introduction

Converting documentation from DITA to AsciiDoc can simplify workflows and improve content accessibility.

Conversion Process

The table below compares key differences between DITA and AsciiDoc.

Format Advantages Disadvantages
DITA Modular, Reusable Content Complex, Requires Special Tools
AsciiDoc Lightweight, Easy to Use Less Standardized
Why Convert from DITA to AsciiDoc?

AsciiDoc is simpler, requires fewer tools, and integrates well with modern CI/CD workflows.

⚠️ Warning: Ensure all DITA topics are well-structured before conversion to prevent content loss.

Conclusion

By switching to AsciiDoc, teams can streamline their documentation processes while maintaining clarity and flexibility.
